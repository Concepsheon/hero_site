Volunteer

HERO is incredibly grateful for the many volunteers that generoulsy donate their time to help us each year. It is a great way to give back to those in need. Anything and everything helps.

If you or your organization are interested in volunteering, please get in touch with us to schedule your visit. We hope to see you soon!

FAQ

What will I be doing?

Volunteers who work with HERO perform a variety of tasks. Painting, cleaning, framing and roofing are all common assignments for volunteers. If you have a special skill to share, please let us know so a plan can be made to put your skill to use! No construction experience is needed to volunteer with HERO. Contact us to find out more about the many opportunities available for volunteers.

Where will I stay?

Usually all volunteers stay in our bunkhouse, a two-story living quarters with 12 beds, two bathrooms, and a full kitchen. HERO asks for a $10 per night donation per guest to cover the cost of utilities and maintenance.

What should I bring?

Volunteers should wear clothes that they don't mind getting dirty and closed toed, hard soled shoes. There are two grocery stores and a handful of restaurants within walking distance from HERO. Much of Greensboro can be covered by foot, but alternate forms of transportation, like a bike or skateboard, are also a great way to get around.
